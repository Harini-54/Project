AMRITA VISHWA VIDHYAPEETAM

Amrita vishwa vidhyapeeetam is offering a science symposium on this saturday in which the students should participate like teams and have to create a project based on the topic given by us.

The topic here is "The Classical Education for the Future". Future generations will find this to be a very useful topic, and our team also believed that it would provide students with knowledge of current events and the precautions that must be taken for the sake of the next generation in order to preserve their society, education, etc. This is the main reason we put together this symposium.

Participation:

Students who are studying 4th year can also participate along with the other years of the students. Students can participate as a team and the team should consist of maximum 3-5 members.

Selection:

This symposium is a National wide competition conducted by our institution where each participant is required to make project about a given topic or area, and then present it in front of the juries. The jury will look into the work done by the groups or the individual participants. Once the topics are given you then, the partcipants have one or two weeks to sumbit their work. After the completeion of the givn time, Participants have to sumbit their work and present it. In this symposium , we conduct 4 events and the students can participate in any of the four based on their intrests. The 4 events are: Hackathon, Web designing , Paper sumbission , Project.
